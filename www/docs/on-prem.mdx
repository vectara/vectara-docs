A private deployment of Vectara brings the full power of our Retrieval-Augmented 
Generation (RAG) platform directly into your controlled environment. This deployment 
option enables organizations to leverage cutting-edge AI technology while maintaining 
complete control over their data, infrastructure, and security posture.
